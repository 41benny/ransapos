3. DESIGN SYSTEM YANG DITERAPKAN
================================================================================

A. LAYOUT STRUCTURE
   Before (Bootstrap):
   <div class="container-fluid py-4">
     <div class="row">
       <div class="col-12">
         <div class="card">
           <div class="card-body">

   After (Tailwind):
   <div class="container mx-auto px-4 py-6">
     <div class="bg-white rounded-xl shadow-md p-6">

B. STATISTICS CARDS
   Before (Bootstrap):
   <div class="card">
     <div class="card-body">
       <div class="icon bg-gradient-primary">

   After (Tailwind):
   <div class="bg-gradient-to-br from-blue-500 to-blue-600 rounded-xl shadow-md p-6 text-white">
     <div class="bg-white bg-opacity-30 rounded-full p-3">

C. FORM INPUTS
   Before (Bootstrap):
   <input class="form-control @error('field') is-invalid @enderror">
   <div class="invalid-feedback">{{ $message }}</div>

   After (Tailwind):
   <input class="w-full px-4 py-2 border border-gray-300 rounded-lg
                  focus:ring-2 focus:ring-blue-500 focus:border-transparent
                  @error('field') border-red-500 @enderror">
   <p class="text-red-500 text-xs mt-1">{{ $message }}</p>

D. BUTTONS
   Before (Bootstrap):
   <button class="btn btn-primary">Save</button>
   <a class="btn btn-secondary">Cancel</a>

   After (Tailwind):
   <button class="px-6 py-2 bg-gradient-to-r from-blue-500 to-cyan-600
                  hover:from-blue-600 hover:to-cyan-700 text-white
                  rounded-lg shadow-md transition-all duration-150">
     Save
   </button>
   <a class="px-4 py-2 bg-gray-500 hover:bg-gray-600 text-white
             rounded-lg transition-colors duration-150">
     Cancel
   </a>

E. TABLES
   Before (Bootstrap):
   <table class="table table-hover">
     <thead>
       <tr>
         <th class="text-uppercase">

   After (Tailwind):
   <table class="min-w-full divide-y divide-gray-200">
     <thead class="bg-gray-50">
       <tr>
         <th class="px-4 py-3 text-left text-xs font-medium text-gray-500
                    uppercase tracking-wider">

F. ALERTS
   Before (Bootstrap):
   <div class="alert alert-success alert-dismissible">
     <button type="button" class="close" data-dismiss="alert">

   After (Tailwind):
   <div class="bg-green-50 border border-green-200 text-green-700
               px-4 py-3 rounded-lg">

G. BADGES
   Before (Bootstrap):
   <span class="badge bg-success">Active</span>

   After (Tailwind):
   <span class="inline-block px-3 py-1 bg-green-100 text-green-700
                rounded-full text-xs font-medium">Active</span>
