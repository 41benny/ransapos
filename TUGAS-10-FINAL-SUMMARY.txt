✅ 2. HPP AUTO-TRACKING

Flow:
   1. Customer beli produk di POS
   2. StockService::reduceSaleStock() called
   3. System:
      - Ambil product.purchase_price sebagai unit_cost
      - Calculate: total_cost = unit_cost × quantity
      - Save to stock_mutations:
         * mutation_type: 'out'
         * unit_cost: dari purchase_price
         * total_cost: calculated
   4. HPP tercatat otomatis ✅

Example:
   Product: Espresso
   - Purchase Price: Rp 8.000
   - Selling Price: Rp 15.000
   
   Sale: 2 cups
   - Revenue: 2 × 15.000 = Rp 30.000
   - COGS: 2 × 8.000 = Rp 16.000 (auto-calculated)
   - Gross Profit: Rp 14.000

      * Total Biaya

   Report Structure:
      A. PENDAPATAN
         - Penjualan: dari sales.grand_total
         - Total: Sum revenue
      
      B. HARGA POKOK PENJUALAN (HPP)
         - HPP: dari stock_mutations.total_cost (type=out)
      
      C. LABA KOTOR
         - = Pendapatan - HPP
         - Margin %
      
      D. BIAYA OPERASIONAL
         - Grouped by COA group
         - Detail per account
         - Subtotal per group
         - Total biaya
      
      E. LABA BERSIH
         - = Laba Kotor - Biaya Operasional
         - Margin %
   
   Actions:
      - Print report
      - Export (future: PDF/Excel)

Calculation Source:
   - Revenue: SELECT SUM(grand_total) FROM sales
   - COGS: SELECT SUM(total_cost) FROM stock_mutations WHERE type='out'
   - Expenses: SELECT SUM(amount) FROM cash_transactions 
               WHERE type='out' AND coa_account_id IS NOT NULL
               GROUP BY coa_account_id

4. Submit → COA tersimpan
5. Siap digunakan untuk transaksi ✅

✅ Flow 2: Transaksi Penjualan → HPP Tercatat

1. Kasir login → buka POS
2. Customer beli:
   - 2 Espresso @ Rp 15.000 = Rp 30.000
   - 1 Cappuccino @ Rp 20.000 = Rp 20.000
   - Total: Rp 50.000
3. Kasir proses pembayaran
4. System:
   - Save sale (revenue: Rp 50.000)
   - Reduce stock
   - Calculate HPP:
      * Espresso: 2 × 8.000 = Rp 16.000
      * Cappuccino: 1 × 10.000 = Rp 10.000
      * Total COGS: Rp 26.000
   - Save to stock_mutations dengan cost
5. HPP tercatat otomatis ✅

✅ Flow 3: Catat Biaya Operasional

1. Admin login → "Transaksi Kas"
2. Klik "Catat Transaksi Baru"
3. Form:
   - Akun: Bank BCA
   - Jenis: Kas Keluar
   - COA dropdown muncul (required)
   - Pilih: 6-120 Biaya Listrik & Air
   - Jumlah: Rp 2.000.000
   - Tanggal: hari ini
   - Deskripsi: Pembayaran listrik bulan November
4. Submit → Transaction saved:
   - cash_account_id: Bank BCA
   - coa_account_id: 6-120
   - type: out
   - amount: 2.000.000
5. Saldo bank berkurang
6. Biaya tercatat untuk laporan ✅

✅ Flow 4: Generate Laporan Laba Rugi

1. Admin login → "Laporan Laba Rugi"
2. Filter:
   - Dari: 01 Nov 2025
   - Sampai: 30 Nov 2025
   - Outlet: (All)
3. Klik "Tampilkan"
4. System calculate:
   
   PENDAPATAN:
   - Query sales dalam periode
   - Total Revenue: Rp 50.000.000 (contoh)
   
   HPP:
   - Query stock_mutations OUT dalam periode
   - Total COGS: Rp 26.000.000
   
   LABA KOTOR:
   - = 50.000.000 - 26.000.000 = Rp 24.000.000
   - Margin: 48%
   
   BIAYA OPERASIONAL:
   - Group BIAYA OPERASIONAL:
      * 6-100 Gaji: Rp 10.000.000
      * 6-120 Listrik: Rp 2.000.000
      * ... dst
      * Subtotal: Rp 15.000.000
   - Total Biaya: Rp 15.000.000
   
   LABA BERSIH:
   - = 24.000.000 - 15.000.000 = Rp 9.000.000
   - Margin: 18%

5. Laporan ditampilkan dengan format terstruktur
6. Klik "Print" untuk cetak ✅

✅ Flow 5: Validasi & Business Rules

Validasi COA:
   - Transaksi keluar WAJIB pilih COA ✅

16. TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Q: COA dropdown tidak muncul?
A: 1. Check type = 'out'
   2. Check JavaScript loaded (F12 console)
   3. Refresh page

Q: HPP tidak tercatat?
A: 1. Check product.purchase_price terisi
   2. Check StockService::reduceSaleStock() called
   3. Verify stock_mutations.total_cost column exists

Q: Laporan laba rugi kosong?
A: 1. Check ada transaksi dalam periode
   2. Verify date filter correct
   3. Check ada sales/cash transactions

Q: Error "COA wajib dipilih"?
A: Untuk transaksi kas keluar, COA memang wajib.
   Pilih salah satu akun biaya (expense).

Q: Laba bersih negatif (rugi)?
A: Normal jika biaya > laba kotor.
   Review expenses, cari efisiensi.
